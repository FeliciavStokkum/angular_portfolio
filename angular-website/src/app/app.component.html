Shopping cart

For my second-year internship at Depot Software, I worked on creating the shopping cart for an e-commerce website. I was responsible for both designing the cart and making it fully functional. This included adding products to the cart and moving them to the checkout page. I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the front-end features, and C# for the API that was needed to process the data. Throughout the project, I focused on making sure the cart worked well on different devices and browsers. It was a great experience that helped me improve my skills in web development.
